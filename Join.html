MEMBERSHIP

Join Access 2 Justice.

Annual membership is $15 . Your membership helps fund educational programming, community outreach, networking events, advocacy initiatives, and volunteer opportunities throughout the year.

Membership Fee Waiver

Access 2 Justice is committed to ensuring that financial circumstances never prevent students from becoming involved.

Members who are unable to pay the annual membership fee may request a confidential fee waiver by contacting the Executive Team.

  • ✔ Confidential request submitted by email
  • ✔ Reviewed privately by the Executive Team
  • ✔ No proof of income required
  • ✔ Full or partial fee waivers may be granted
  • ✔ Decisions are normally provided within 3–5 business days
