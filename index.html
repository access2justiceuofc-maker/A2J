Access 2 Justice is a student-led movement at the University of Calgary opening the doors of law to everyone — through legal literacy, accessibility, and community advocacy.

OUR CREED

WHAT WE DO

Three pillars. One purpose.

We turn complex legal systems into something every student — and every community member — can navigate with confidence.

📖

Legal Literacy

Workshops, Events, and discussions that translate dense legal concepts into plain language students can actually use.

⚖️

Accessibility

Connecting peers to free clinics, student legal services, and resources.

🔨

Advocacy

Panels and partnerships with practitioners, professors, and community organizations driving systemic change.
